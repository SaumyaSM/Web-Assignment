About Us

At Furry Friends Rescue Society, our passion is to make a difference in the lives of animals who deserve a second chance. Founded with the belief that every animal deserves love, care, and a safe home, we have been dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and finding forever homes for pets since 2003.

What We Do

Rescue and Rehabilitation: We tirelessly work to rescue animals from various situations, including neglect, abuse, abandonment, and overcrowded shelters. Once in our care, we provide them with medical attention, necessary treatments, and a nurturing environment to heal both physically and emotionally.

Foster Care: We rely on our compassionate network of foster families who provide temporary homes for our animals. Foster care allows us to give individual attention, assess their behavior and needs, and prepare them for successful adoptions.

Adoption: Finding forever homes for our rescued animals is our ultimate goal. We carefully screen potential adopters, match them with suitable pets, and facilitate the adoption process to ensure a successful and lifelong bond between the animal and their new family.

Education and Awareness: We believe that education is key to creating a more compassionate society. We conduct workshops, seminars, and outreach programs to raise awareness about responsible pet ownership, animal welfare issues, and the importance of spaying/neutering.

Community Engagement: We actively engage with our local community through various initiatives, such as adoption events, vaccination drives, and collaborations with other organizations. We strive to build a strong support network of animal lovers and advocates who share our mission.

Advocacy: We advocate for animal rights and welfare by working with policymakers, local authorities, and animal welfare organizations. We aim to influence positive change and create a society where animals are treated with compassion and respect.


Our Commitment
  • Ensuring the well-being of every animal in our care.
  • Providing a safe and loving environment throughout their journey with us.
  • Promoting responsible pet ownership and the importance of spaying/neutering.
  • Matching animals with the most suitable adoptive families.
  • Offering ongoing support and resources to our adopters and the community.
How You Can Help

Adopt: Consider adopting a pet and give them a loving home. By adopting, you save a life and open up space for us to rescue another animal in need.

Volunteer: Join our dedicated team of volunteers and make a direct impact on the lives of animals. Whether you assist with animal care, adoption events, or administrative tasks, your time and skills can make a significant difference.

Foster: Open your home to an animal in need by becoming a foster caregiver. Fostering provides temporary care, love, and socialization to help animals prepare for their forever homes.

Donate: Your generous donations help us provide medical care, food, shelter, and enrichment for our animals. Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a difference in their lives.

Spread the Word: Share our mission, adoption success stories, and educational content on social media platforms. Help us raise awareness about the importance of adopting and supporting animal welfare causes.

We are grateful for the unwavering support of our community, volunteers, adopters, and donors who make our work possible. Together, we can create a world where every animal is cherished, protected, and loved.

Thank you for joining us on this compassionate journey towards a brighter future for animals in need.
